Introduction

Galatasaray is a professional football club based in Istanbul, Turkey. It was founded in 1905 by Ali Sami Yen and his fellow students at Galatasaray High School.

History

The club has a rich history and became the first and only Turkish team to win a major UEFA competition by capturing the UEFA Cup in 2000, remaining unbeaten throughout the tournament.
